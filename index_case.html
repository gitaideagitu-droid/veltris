Revenue Cycle Management Solution

Addressing inefficiencies through automation and process optimization

Requirement

A DSO with multiple PMS systems and high call center staff turnover faced significant RCM challenges. Inefficiencies stemmed from staff handling multiple systems, manual claims processing delaying collections, and overdue claims impacting cash flow.

Approach

Veltris provided a comprehensive RCM solution that:

  • Automated claims collection from payers and patients.
  • Removed dependency on manual PMS updates.
  • Standardized claims processing across diverse systems.
  • Improved A/R tracking, follow-up, and denial management.
